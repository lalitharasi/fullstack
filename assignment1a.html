Our Dance Forms

Bharatanatyam (தமிழ் நாட்டியम)

The oldest classical dance form originating from Tamil Nadu.

Kathak (कथक)

Storytelling dance from North India, famous for intricate footwork.

Odissi (ଓଡ଼ିଶୀ)

The dance of Odisha, known for its sculptural poses.

Kuchipudi (కూచిపూడి)

From Andhra Pradesh, combining dance, drama, and music.

Manipuri (মণিপুরী)

Gentle dance from Manipur, known for soft movements.

Mohiniyattam (മോഹിനിയാട്ടം)

The dance of Kerala, characterized by graceful movements.
